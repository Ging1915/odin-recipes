Sandwich

Here you see a delectable snady made from provolone and ham on a wheat bread.

Here's what you'll need

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  1. Set out two pieces of bread
  2. Slap your cold cut of ham on said bread
  3. Gently place your tomato
  4. Same goes for the cheese
  5. Get artisanal with your lettuce and slightly bunch it up
  6. Put on your top bread and there you have it

Now eat your creation
