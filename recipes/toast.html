Toast

Today you will learn how to cook the delicacy known as toast

Get a piece of bread make her warm, lather up some salted milk fat and there you have it.

Items Needed...

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Toaster

Now time for the fun part!

  1. Cut bread from loaf.
  2. Put bread in toaster.
  3. Turn on toaster.
  4. Once toaster pops take bread out and apply butter

Enjoy
